About this tournament

Bring your best deck and battle with your opponents!

From November 11th till November 12th we will be hosting an international Hearthstone tournament in Apeldoorn, The Netherlands.
Everyone over 14 years of age can sign up and the top 3 will win special prices.
People from all over Europe will be participating in on of the greatest Hearthstone events in history.
The format played will be Wild-format, which means all cards are allowed!

Bring your own device!

Even though we will have plenty of PC's set up, we will also have room for you to set up your own device!
Sign up and get a 40% discount for staying at the nearby camping Hertshoorn, which will have an associated Hearthstone theme during this event.

We hope to see you soon!
